Monospace Font Recommendation

  • Criteria:
    • Free and open source.
    • Can be self-hosted for use with offline systems without Internet connectivity.
    • Distinctive characters: Features a clear difference between 0 (zero) and O (letter O), as well as 1 (one), I (capital i), and l (lowercase L).
  • The Courier New and Courier fonts are web safe fonts and come pre-installed on Windows/macOS/Linux systems but do not have distinctive characters.
  • The Consolas font has distinctive characters but is only free on Microsoft Windows, Office and Visual Studio.
