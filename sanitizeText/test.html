Plain Text Editor

The following modifications will be performed automatically when text is typed or pasted into the editor.
  • The following characters will be replaced with a single space each: Carriage return \r, tab \t, en space  , em space  , zero width space >​< (between angle brackets).
  • Multiple consecutive spaces will be reduced to a single space.
  • Beginning and trailing whitespace will not be trimmed as it would prevent spaces from being keyed in.
  • Smart punctuation from Microsoft Word/Excel such as “ ” ″ ‘ ’ ′ ‐ — – will be replaced with their normal ASCII forms " ' -.
